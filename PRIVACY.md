# Privacy Policy for KeepTouch

*Last Updated: January 22, 2025*

## 1. Introduction
KeepTouch ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### a) Contact Information
- We access your device's contacts only with your explicit permission
- We store basic contact information (names, phone numbers) to enable reminder functionality
- Contact data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties

### b) User Account Information
- Email address (for authentication and account management)
- User ID (internal identifier)
- Subscription status and purchase history

### c) User-Generated Data
- Check-in frequencies and reminder settings
- Message history and preferences
- App usage data and interaction patterns

### d) Technical Data
- Device information and identifiers
- App performance data
- Error logs and crash reports
- IP address (for security and fraud prevention)

### e) Analytics and Advertising Data
- Advertising identifier (IDFA) - only collected with your permission
- App interaction events (screen views, button taps)
- Purchase events and subscription status
- App launch and session data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### a) Core App Functionality
- Provide contact reminder services
- Generate AI-powered messages through OpenAI's API
- Send push notifications for reminders
- Process subscription payments

### b) Analytics and Improvement
- Analyze app usage patterns to improve user experience
- Monitor app performance and fix bugs
- Understand feature adoption and user behavior

### c) Advertising and Marketing
- Measure advertising campaign effectiveness
- Optimize ad targeting and attribution
- Track conversion events (app installs, purchases)
- Create aggregated audience insights

### d) Security and Legal
- Maintain app security and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations
- Enforce our Terms of Service

## 4. Third-Party Services

### a) OpenAI
- We use OpenAI's API to generate message suggestions
- Message prompts are processed securely
- No personal contact information is shared with OpenAI

### b) Supabase
- We use Supabase for secure data storage
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Hosted in compliance with industry standards

### c) Facebook/Meta (Analytics & Advertising)
- We use Facebook SDK for advertising attribution and analytics
- Data shared includes:
  - Advertising identifier (IDFA) - only with your consent
  - App events (launches, purchases, registrations)
  - Hashed email for improved ad targeting
- Purpose: To measure ad effectiveness and optimize campaigns
- You can opt-out through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

### d) RevenueCat
- Processes subscription payments
- Manages subscription status
- No payment card details are stored by our app

### e) Sentry
- Collects crash reports and error logs
- Helps us identify and fix app issues
- No personal information is included in crash reports

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in these circumstances:

### a) With Service Providers
- Third-party services listed above for their specified purposes
- Only minimum necessary data is shared

### b) Advertising Partners
- Aggregated and anonymized data for ad performance
- Hashed identifiers for attribution (with your consent)

### c) Legal Requirements
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights or user safety

## 6. Data Storage and Security
- All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- We regularly backup data to prevent loss
- We implement security measures to protect against unauthorized access
- Data retention: We keep your data while your account is active and as required by law

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### a) Access and Control
- Access your personal data through the app
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your data

### b) Opt-Out Options
- Push notifications: iOS Settings > Notifications
- Ad tracking: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
- Analytics: Contact us to opt-out
- Marketing emails: Unsubscribe link in emails

### c) iOS App Tracking Transparency
- We request permission to track for advertising purposes
- You can change this anytime in iOS Settings
- Denying permission doesn't affect core app functionality
